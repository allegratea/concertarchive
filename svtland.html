HISTORY

Discovering SEVENTEEN completely transformed my perspective on live music and performance. From tracking their self-producing journey in the early days to witnessing their incredible synchronization live on stage, being a Carat has been an unforgettable experience. This archive tracks the milestones, energy, and memories from the various tour stops I've been lucky enough to attend over the years.
