KCON

Attending KCON was an absolutely unforgettable experience. The energy in the arena was electric from the moment the pre-show started, and the massive ocean of glowing lightsticks from so many different fandoms lighting up the crowd was breathtaking. Seeing a lineup of world-class K-pop artists deliver flawless synchronization, incredible live vocals, and charismatic stage presence exceeded every expectation. It was easily one of the best weekends ever, and getting VIP access for the convention panels, artist engagements, and pre-show soundchecks made every single second worth it!
